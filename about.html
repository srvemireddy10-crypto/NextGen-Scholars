About Us

NextGen Scholars is a student-led academic journal committed to providing a professional platform for young researchers to share their discoveries, insights, and original ideas with the world. Our mission is to empower high school and early college students by showcasing their work in a space that values rigor, creativity, and collaboration. We believe that age should never be a barrier to contribution, and through peer engagement and academic publishing, we are fostering the next wave of scholars and innovators.

Our Mission

At NextGen Scholars, our mission is to break down barriers to academic publishing by empowering high school and early-college students to share their research with the world. We strive to cultivate intellectual growth, encourage curiosity, and amplify youth voices across disciplines. By providing a platform for peer-reviewed student work, we hope to inspire the next generation of scholars, thinkers, and changemakers.
